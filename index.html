What is Smart Glasses ?

En basit tabirle...

Smart glasses are smart devices that are created by combining optical glasses that people routinely use with electronic hardware and adding software and algorithms into them.

1/3

Considering the purpose of use and interaction with the user, it is considered in the wearable technology class.

2/3

Although smart glasses are basically considered to be in the same class as other wearable technology products, they have much greater potential due to the functions they can do and the wider purpose of use. The fact that the features of smart glasses released today, their abilities, their intended use and designs are very different from each other supports this situation.

How can the use of smart glasses be supported?

AI

Images from the Smart Glasses camera can be processed with AI models. Thus, an autonomous computer vision becomes innovative.

Mobile platform

Sharing data from mobile applications with smart glasses creates more interaction and increases the quality of solutions offered by smart glasses.

Desktop platform

To be able to see, authorize, communicate, encrypt, etc. all smart glasses used actively or passively on the computer in real time.

Cloud entegration

The integrated operation of smart glasses with cloud systems will make the function more useful. Smart glasses can represent a critical threshold in the efficient use of the IoT platform.
